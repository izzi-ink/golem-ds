# Three Pillars of JS

The following concepts are regarded as the **Three Pillars of JS** and are the foundation of the language:

- **Types / Coercion**
- **Scope / Closures**
- **this / Prototypes**

## Learning the Three Pillars

You can learn more about these concepts in the following links:
